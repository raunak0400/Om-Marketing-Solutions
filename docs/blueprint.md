# **App Name**: Apex Digital

## Core Features:

- Web Development Showcase: Display a portfolio of completed web development projects with detailed case studies demonstrating measurable results.
- Automation Solutions Overview: Describe available automation systems and their benefits for streamlining business processes. Offer a tool that enables the user to calculate the potential ROI of specific automations.
- Business Software Integrations: Highlight pre-built integrations with popular business software platforms to enhance compatibility and ease of use.
- Digital Transformation Roadmap: Provide a guided roadmap outlining the steps involved in digital transformation, with resource links and downloadable templates.
- Client Testimonials: Feature client testimonials (text and video) with clear identification (title and company name) to add a sense of trustworthiness
- Lead Capture Forms: Strategic placement of lead capture forms throughout the website, offering valuable resources in exchange for contact information.
- Service Cost Estimation: Generative AI powered tool to give clients a general estimate on how much specific services would cost.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3066BE) to convey stability and corporate professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F2F5), almost white to offer a sense of simplicity and clarity.
- Accent color: Vibrant purple (#904AE3) for calls to action and interactive elements. 
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern, tech-forward appearance.
- Use flat, minimalist icons to represent services and solutions.
- Emphasize whitespace to create a clean and modern feel. Use a grid-based layout for a sense of order.
- Incorporate subtle animations on scrolling or hover states to enhance engagement without distracting from content.
